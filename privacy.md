# Dreamstack — Privacy policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-24_

This policy describes what data Dreamstack collects and why.

## Data we collect

- **Device identifiers & crash logs** — via Firebase Crashlytics, only to
  diagnose crashes. Stored under an anonymous user ID we generate locally.
- **Event analytics** — via Firebase Analytics. Used to understand which
  features are played and where players get stuck. Never used to identify
  individuals.
- **Advertising ID** — only when you accept personalized ads. If you
  decline, we pass the non-personalized flag to AdMob.

## Data we do NOT collect

- Your name, email, phone number, address.
- Photos, contacts, location.
- Any information that would identify you personally.

## Third parties

- Google Firebase (analytics, crash reporting, remote config)
- Google AdMob (advertising)
- Google Play Billing / Apple StoreKit (in-app purchases)

Each of them has its own privacy terms at google.com/policies and
apple.com/legal.

## Your rights

You can revoke tracking consent any time from the game's Settings → Privacy.
